Engage Tank | 1H Mace (Caller)
Weapon
Q: Sacred Ground. Used to silence and slow targets post-clump and allows for some level of peel.
W: Air Compressor. Your main engage ability. Used to clump enemies together for DPS.
E: Deep Leap. Used in conjunction with your W to stun clumped enemies at the time they are pulled together.
Passive: Energetic. Used to regain mana in fights of attrition.
Armor
R: Force Shield. Used directly after clump to ensure your survival. Increases resistances and healing to those inside the bubble.
Passive: Toughness. Used to gain additional resistances.
Helmet
D: Ice Block. Used to ensure your survival. Negates all damage and CC effects while active.
Passive: Efficiency. Allows you to save on mana while casting abilities.
Boots(Base T6 Min)
F: Break Free. Used to dash out of CCs if you happen to get caught.
Passive: Quick Thinker. Reduces cooldowns.
Consumables
Food: 8.1 Beef Sandwich. The additional health is to add to your survivability.
Potion: Gigantify Potion. Gives you a short boost to max HP and immunity to forced movement.
