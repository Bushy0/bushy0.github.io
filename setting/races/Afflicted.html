Afflicted

Afflicted

Cursed a hundred years ago by the dark mage, Zayne, the Afflicted, sometimes called zombie as a slur, live life between the forces of light and dark. Native to the Vascana Republic, the Afflicted have adopted a democratic way of living.


Stats


Ability Score Increase

Your Constitution score increases by 2, and one other ability score of your choice increase by 1.


Age

Afflicted reach maturity by age 16. Afflicted live about as long as humans do, 80 years.


Alignment

Most Afflicted are good and rarely choose sides when it comes to law and chaos.


Size

Afflicted are about 5-6ft feet tall. Your size is Medium.


Speed

Your base walking speed is 25 feet.


Darkvision

You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.


Cold to the Touch

You know the cantrip Chill Touch. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it.


Undead Nature

Due to their undead nature, Afflicted are resistant to necrotic damage and weak to radiant damage. Afflicted also do not have to sleep, but must rest for at least 4 hours.


Languages

You can speak, read, and write Common and Abyssal.
