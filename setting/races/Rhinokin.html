Rhinokin

Rhinokin

Rhinokin are a race of nomadic peoples flung across the savannahs, higher mountain steppes and prairie terrains. They are believed to be ancestors of modern rhinos who did not believe in dropping to all fours and becoming simple animals. According to their history, there were once no rhinos, only rhinokin. Many rhinokin suffered at the hands of other races for a long time. Humans and other greedy races would poach them for their ivory horns.


Stats


Ability Score Increase

Your Strength score increases by 2, and your Constitution score increases by 1.


Age

Rhinokin have lifespans comparable to humans. They enter adulthood in their late teens and usually live less than a century.


Alignment

Rhinokin value strength and follow a strict code of laws. They tend to stay out of external affairs. Most Rhinokin are Lawful neutral.


Size

Rhinokin are between 7 and 8 feet tall and weigh between 280 and 340 pounds. Your size is Medium.


Speed

Your base walking speed is 30 feet.


Natural Athlete

You have proficiency in the Athletics skill.


Horn Charge

Once per long rest, you can charge in a straight line up to double your move speed. Any creature caught in your charge makes a DC 13 DEX saving throw or suffer 1d6 bludgeoning damage and are knocked prone. After you use this trait, you can’t use it again until you finish a short or long rest.


Powerful Build

You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.


Fire Birth

You have resistance to fire damage.


Languages

You can speak, read, and write Common and Dwarven.
