Rules

Races

You can play any race from the PHB. You can also play the follow additional races: Aarakocra, Tabaxi, Goliath, Kenku, Rhinokin, and Afflicted. Variant Humans can't take a feat at level 4.


Alignment

No evil characters. Besides that, I do not have a preference for your alignment. If you decide to play Chaotic Neutral, then play CHAOTIC NEUTRAL, not Chaotic Stupid(basically don't be a murder hobo and play a character that WANTS to adventure).


Classes

No artificers and no Wild Magic Sorcerer. Other than that, any class is allowed to be played as long it is from an official non-setting DnD BOOK. No Unearthed Arcana classes or subclasses unless approved by me.


Feats

The Lucky feat is prohibited. Any others are allowed.


NPCs and Monsters

Monsters antagonize the characters, but the DM does not. Meaning I am not out to get you. I am not the players’ opponent. I want you to succeed. I will be running monsters convincingly because THEY want to defeat the characters. This goes with hostile NPCs as well.


Pacing

I will be keeping the pace of the game. Meaning things can happen while you are planning things or if things get too slow i'll get things back on track. It's okay to joke around at the table, but when I start to move things forward, out of respect for me and the game, players need to settle down so we can continue forward.


Judgment Calls

Keep rules lawyering to a bare minimum. I will have the final say on things regardless of the rules. If I make a bad call on something or didn't get the ruling right based on RAW please refrain from interrupting the flow of the game to tell me and wait until a break or the end of the session. Alternatively, private message me and I’ll take note of it for when a similar situation comes up.


Making Actions

I want you to be thinking, "who is my character, what would they actually do, why do they care about what’s happening, what are their strengths and flaws." Describe what your character is doing. Rather than "I roll stealth" or "I attack with my bow", say "I crouch in the grass and move slow and quietly towards the orcs" or "I nock an arrow in my bow and I aim for the orc’s heart". You TELL me what you are doing, and I'll say what to roll or if to roll.


Speaking

Try to keep out of game conversations to a minimum. Talking about the game is fine so long as you aren't interrupting the acting player and/or DM. When speaking to NPCs or each other’s characters, speak IN-GAME. Talk to them as if you would talk to someone IRL, through your character(Voices are not necessary but are encouraged). The exception goes for actions such as "I want to sing to the guard to distract them. Instead of physically singing something in character you can just make a performance check. (Playing instruments is allowed if one is available at the time).


The Meta Game

Player knowledge will always be separate from character knowledge. I will not tolerate meta gaming in my game. This includes telling players what abilities they have or actions they can or can't do (Unless it was roleplayed out). If the player doesn't think of it or realize what they can do, their character won't think of it either. This obviously does not apply to new players who may need a helping hand.


Failure and Death

Failure and death will always be a risk. Without this, the game becomes less challenging and rewarding for completing an encounter. I will not hold back on damage or "pull punches". Meaning I am not afraid to have a monster or NPC kill a player. If a character does die, the player can make a new character of the same level.


The only times you can re-roll dice is if the die lands on the floor and not the table itself. A "cocked die" will be determined by me whether or not it needs to be re-rolled. Do NOT reroll without consulting me else ill assume the rolled was failing. All Dice Rolls Must Be Visible to the DM at all times.


Respect

This is in regards to respecting other players at the table. Some of you may not like sitting and waiting for people as they RP with one another, but at least show them the respect they deserve. Listen to them or at least act interested. I won’t tolerate people interrupting or checking out just because the spotlight isn’t on them or they find the dialog to be boring. I am fine if you want to check out when I am talking(as long as it doesn’t slow the game down of course, however, you need to respect your fellow players.


PvP

Player vs Player conflict is prohibited unless consented by both players. This includes but is not limited to; Attacking, stealing from them, or placing them in harm’s way intentionally.


Sexual Content

All sex scenes fade to black and no non-consensual sexual actions period.


Know Your Character

Know what your abilities, spells, etc do. If you plan to cast a spell for example ‘hold person’ you should know that it is a wisdom saving throw and what your spell save DC.
